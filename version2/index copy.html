After Animal Attack

Step 1: Immediate Care

  • Clean the wound with running water and soap.
  • Apply antiseptic and cover with sterile dressing.

Step 2: Seek Medical Help

  • Go to the nearest hospital or clinic.
  • Bring vaccination and insurance info for faster processing.
